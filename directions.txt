Directions:

##Objective/scoring
- Avoid enemies hitting your ship by shooting bullets at them.
- Score points by destroying enemies.
- Game ends when an enemy reaches your ship.
- Each enemy you hit is worth 10 points.
Good luck!

##Controls
- Left arrow/A key: Moves ship to the left.
- Right arrow/D ley: Moves ship to the right.
- Space bar/Left click (on mouse): Fires bullets.
- ESC: Brings up the pause menu where you can then resume the game, give up, or go 
back to the main menu.
